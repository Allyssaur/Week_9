My Goal

As a collector and life-long Pokemon fan it should seem obvious that my ultimate goal is to "catch 'em all". I am so close to my goal that I can almost count the number of Pokemon left on one hand! Not only doing a living Dex, I am also doing an alternate forms living Dex. I have most alternate gender forms already and I'm getting rather close with my Vivillon Patterns. I am still missing some miscellaneous alternate forms but I am slowly working through some older games to obtain them. I haven't started much on my Pikachu Hat or Alcremie hunts but I will be starting those after I finish the base Dex.


If you would like to see a full report of my progress towards my goal, please feel free to visit my full spreadsheet that I do keep up-to-date on a regular basis!
